Looking for PhD student

We are seeking a PhD student funded by an NSF project in 3D City modeling and visualization. The student will be enrolled in Geospatial Computer Science Ph.D. program or Coastal Marine Science Ph.D. program at Texas A&M University- Corpus Christi (TAMU-CC). The candidate will work with Dr. Lucy Huang from the Department of Computing Science at TAMU-CC and Dr. Xinyue Ye from the Department of Landscape Architecture and Urban Planning and Institute of Data Science at TAMU. The successful candidate will become an NSF CREST Center Fellow at TAMU-CC and receive 1) graduate research assistantship, (b) tuition and fees coverage, (c) support for summer research experience at one collaborator site outside TAMU-CC, (d) travel support for conferences or professional meetings, and other support.

The status of U.S. citizen of underrepresented minorities (African Americans, American Indians including Native Alaskans, Hispanics, and Native Pacific Islanders) is required. A solid programing background is required. Preference will be given to candidates with research experience in computer science, GIS, remote sensing, or urban planning. Applicants should contact Dr. Lucy Huang (lucy.huang@tamucc.edu) and Dr. Xinyue Ye (xinyue.ye@tamu.edu) using "NSF Graduate Assistantship" as the email subject. To apply, please include: Curriculum Vita; Unofficial Copies of Transcripts, GRE scores, Research statement, One representative publication, and Contact information for three references.

Expected start date will be Fall 2022 or Spring 2023, or otherwise agreed. Please contact us for more information.

We invite you to discover the Island University (TAMU-CC), the only university in the nation located on its own island, at the heart of the Texas Gulf Coast! It is also within the easy driving distance to TAMU, Houston, and San Antonio!
